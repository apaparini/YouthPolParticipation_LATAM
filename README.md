Script and tables used for the presentation *"Are young women less politically engaged than young men? Revisiting and discussing Grasso & Smith (2022)"*, prepared for the course 15081 â€“ The Political Economy of Gender Inequality.

- **do-file_WVS** contains the full script with the cleaning and recoding of the World Values Survey dataset, model specifications and analysis and graphs.

- **table_LATAM_integrated** is the output of the LATAM integrated d-table, with average percentages per gender, total and t-tests. The subsequent tabs combine the outputs of the d-table loops for country-specific averages.

- **Variables** contains the summary of variables and dimensions used by the authors for operationalizing "political participation" in its political, community and online dimensions; "political engagement", in its dimensions of political information, efficacy and democratic attitudes, and the remaining control variables.

- the **World Values Survey**, Wave 7 (2017-2022), V6, can be extracted from https://www.worldvaluessurvey.org/WVSDocumentationWV7.jsp

- Grasso & Smith (2022) original database and codebook can be accessed through EURYKA's project official repository: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/727025/results
